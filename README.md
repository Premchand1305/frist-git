A simple interest calculator that computes interest based on principal, rate, and time.
Simple Interest = (Principal × Rate × Time) / 100
- **Principal**: The initial amount of money
- **Rate**: Annual interest rate (in %)
- **Time**: Duration in years
- Principal = $1000
- Rate = 5%
- Time = 3 years
- Simple Interest = (1000 × 5 × 3) / 100 = **$150**
